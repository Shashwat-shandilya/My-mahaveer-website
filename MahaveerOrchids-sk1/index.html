About Mahaveer Orchids


Mahaveer Orchids is a premium residential project offering 2 BHK and 3 BHK homes for buyers in tandem with several contemporary facilities and amenities. The project consists of a total of 490 apartments which are equipped with 24 hour generator backup. The entire campus is always under security cover throughout the day and night while the project also offers several locational advantages to residents. The project spreads over an area of 6.75 acres while average home sizes range between 465 and 1315 sq ft here. Some of the top amenities on offer include a gymnasium, playing zone for kids, swimming pool, multipurpose room, intercom facilities, indoor game facilities, jogging track, cafeteria, library, basketball court, badminton court, skating rink and tennis court. The project is located in close proximity to the Electronic City which is one of the largest tech parks in the country. The project is also located in close proximity to places like HSR Layout, Bommanahalli, BTM Layout, Begur, Madiwala and Harlur. The HAL Bangalore International Airport is located only 14.7 kilometres away from the project and can be accessed through the Outer Ring Road.

This apartment is a project located at very prime location of south- east Bangalore. This apartment is filled with plenty of amenities and surrounded with full of natural beauty. This project was developed by a prominent ISO 9001:2015 certified company named Reddy Structures Private Limited which is known for finest home and quality architecture. This apartment is having 490 flats of different sizes of 1 BHK, 2 BHK and 3 BHK range between 470 and 1315 sqft spreads over an area of 6.75 acres.
